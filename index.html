A quick Introduction to me.

I recived my B.A. in Anthropology with a Minor in Sociology from Texas Tech University in 2019.

  • 3 Years of Museum Expierience with both the Buddy Holly Center and Silent wings Museums.
  • Began working with the texas Tech University Libraries in 2020.
  • Spent the first 4 months as a Library Associate before beign promoted to Evening Section Supervisor.
  • Currently serve on multiple committees including, Leadership Team, Staff Senetor of PR and Caregivers, and serve as Alum. Advisor for an MGC FSL Organization on campus.

I have experience from participation in an archaeological dig in Romoania in 2018, upcoming attendence to both the Joint COnference of Librarians of Color and IFLA in the Netherlands. Growing knoweldge of Museums, Academic LIbraris and Supervisory/Manageral skills.

My Mission

To curate pop-up shows for artists of all mediums including performace, music and damce in both under represented areas and local and up and coming performers.

My Plan

To complete my LIS degree and begin working towards independent curation while either continuing to work within Academic librarianship or Archives/Special Collections.

My Vision

Is to work internationally as a representative for a particular company. I will apply to both this company in South Korea and another in Thailand. Current languages known: English, Spanish and studying with a tutor to prepare for taking the TOPIK test in the future (Korean).
